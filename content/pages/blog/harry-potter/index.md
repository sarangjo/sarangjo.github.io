title: Harry Potter
date: "2019-05-29"
---

Harry Potter is, with no exceptions, the greatest series ever written. JK Rowling has somehow created something that is at the same time a crucial part of my childhood growing up, my go-to refuge no matter the circumstance, and also a guide for living life.

This is one of the first things that I wrote down when I was preparing a list of topics that I could write about for my blog, just because there's so much to write about and so many thoughts and feelings I have about it. I know that there is nothing new or unique I can add to the seemingly limitless compendium of Harry Potter analysis and commentary, but hey this isn't supposed to be a place for anything new or unique, so I'm in the clear.

The scene with Neville's parents in St. Mongo's in _Phoenix_ never fails to jerk a tear from my eye.
